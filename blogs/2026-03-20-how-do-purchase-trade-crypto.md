To start trading, you must fund your account by transferring cash from a Connected SoFi Account (SoFi Checking and Savings). We then convert this cash into a stablecoin (such as USDC) to execute the trade. Please be aware that stablecoins are not issued or guaranteed by SoFi Bank or the FDIC and may lose value.
